Mission

It is our mission to enable dog owners by giving them the necessary tools for avoiding and dealing with unwanted dog behavior. We provide hands-on dog training while giving owners an easy-to-follow structure for training and maintaining the basics in obedience to new dogs and removing unwanted behaviors from the misfits. Our goal is to improve the life you and your family has with your dog.

Services

1-On-1 Sessions

The 1-on-1 option is for owners who want to have a more structured approach to training their dog. Each 1-on-1 session, your dog will progress to performing the commands sit, down, place, heel, and recall. Progressions can vary from dog to dog, depending on what stage their obedience and behavior is at so we want to offer owners the most flexible options for training their dog. 1-on-1 sessions come in bundles of two sessions and four sessions. The ultimate goal of 1-on-1 sessions is for you and your dog to be comfortably integrated into each other's daily lives.

Virtual Consults

Virtual consults are for anyone! Whether you are new to the training methods or a seasoned practitioner. Virtual consults are great for those starting out and unsure where or how to proceed or those that just need some added help or to bounce ideas off someone they trust.
